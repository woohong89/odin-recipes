Homemade Biscuits

Fluffy, buttery, and tender homemade biscuits that are easy to make from scratch. Perfect for breakfast or as a side for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (260g) all-purpose flour, spooned and leveled
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons (85g) cold butter
  • 3/4 cup + 2 tablespoons (207ml) whole milk or buttermilk

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt
  3. Cut cold butter into small pieces and add to flour mixture
  4. Use your fingers or a pastry cutter to work butter into flour until mixture looks like coarse crumbs
  5. Pour in milk and stir until dough comes together
  6. Turn dough onto floured surface and pat to 3/4 inch thickness
  7. Cut with biscuit cutter and place on baking sheet
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown
