Banana Bread

Description

This is a moist and delicious banana bread recipe that uses up those overripe bananas perfectly. It's easy to make and loved by everyone!

Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or dessert. The riper your bananas, the better the flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan
  3. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork
  4. Mix in the melted butter
  5. Stir in baking soda and salt
  6. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract
  7. Add the flour and mix until just combined (don't overmix!)
  8. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan
  9. Bake for 60-65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
  10. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack
