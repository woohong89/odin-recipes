Golden Sweet Cornbread

Fluffy, buttery, and tender homemade biscuits that are easy to make from scratch. Perfect for breakfast or as a side for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Grease a 9x9 inch baking pan
  3. In a large bowl, mix together flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  4. In another bowl, whisk together milk, oil, and egg
  5. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined
  6. Pour batter into prepared pan
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown
